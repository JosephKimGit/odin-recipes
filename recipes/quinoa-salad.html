Quinoa Salad

Description

A delicious and filling dish of choice for health-conscious people. It is packed with protein and a good source of fibre. It can be light enough for breakfast and hearty enough for dinner as a side.

This flexible dish can be customizable to your tastes which is why this is just one example of the many combinations one can create with quinoa. And it can be made incredibly quickly.

The beauty is in its simplicity!

Ingredients

  • Quinoa
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Measure out 750mL of water and bring it to a boil
  2. Pour in 250mL of quinoa into the boiling water and lower the heat to low
  3. Cover the pot and wait approx. 15 minutes
  4. In a seperate bowl, put in the diced tomato, cucumber, parsley, onion, and olive oil and mix together
  5. Once the quinoa is cooked, run it through with a fork to fluff it up
  6. Mix the quinoa together with the vegetable mixture and add salt/pepper as necessary
  7. Serve the desired amount and refergerate the rest for later consumption
